# Korean AI — Overview

Korea's sovereign AI ecosystem — chaebol-backed and startup models built for Korean language, data, and national independence from US AI infrastructure.

## Why Sovereign AI

Korea's sovereign AI push is driven by:
- **Language** — Korean morphology is poorly served by models trained on English-dominant data
- **Data sovereignty** — enterprise and government data must stay on domestic infrastructure
- **Strategic independence** — avoid critical dependence on US providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google)
- **Competitiveness** — national AI capability as economic and defense priority

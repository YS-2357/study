# EXAONE

## What It Is

LG AI Research's open-weight Korean language model. Released publicly — available for download and local deployment. Strong performance on Korean language tasks and coding benchmarks.

## Why It Matters

Unlike HyperCLOVA X (API-only) and Samsung Gauss (internal-only), EXAONE is open-weight — anyone can download and run it. This makes it the go-to for Korean researchers and developers who want a domestic model without vendor lock-in.

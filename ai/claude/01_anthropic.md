# Anthropic

## What It Is

Anthropic is an AI safety and research company founded in January 2021. Its mission: "the responsible development and maintenance of advanced AI for the long-term benefit of humanity." Structured as a Public Benefit Corporation with a Long-Term Benefit Trust in its governance.

## Founding Story

Eight co-founders left OpenAI in 2021 over a fundamental disagreement on safety philosophy and scaling strategy — not over the Microsoft deal as often reported.

| Founder | Role at Anthropic |
|---------|------------------|
| Dario Amodei | CEO (former VP of Research at OpenAI) |
| Daniela Amodei | President (former VP of Safety & Policy at OpenAI) |
| Jared Kaplan | Chief Science Officer |
| Sam McCandlish | Chief Architect |
| Tom Brown | Co-founder, Research |
| Jack Clark | Co-founder, Policy |
| Christopher Olah | Co-founder, Interpretability Research Lead |
| Benjamin Mann | Co-founder, Head of Labs |

> Dario Amodei: *"The real reason we left was a difference in vision about how to approach AI safety."*

All seven co-founders pledged 80% of their combined fortunes (~$21B+) to combat AI-driven inequality (2025).

## Business Model

Primary revenue: **pay-per-token API calls** (~70–75% of revenue). Customers pay per million tokens (input + output), tiered by model. Additional streams: Claude Code, enterprise contracts, cloud platform partnerships (AWS Bedrock, Google Cloud Vertex AI, Microsoft Foundry).

**Revenue growth:**

| Date | Annual Run Rate |
|------|----------------|
| Jan 2025 | ~$1B |
| Aug 2025 | ~$5B |
| Feb 2026 | ~$14B |

Claude Code alone: >$2.5B run-rate (2026). Over 500 customers spending $1M+ annually.

## Funding

Total raised: **$67B+** across all rounds.

| Round | Date | Amount | Valuation |
|-------|------|--------|-----------|
| Series A | May 2021 | $124M | $550M |
| Series B | Apr 2022 | $580M | $4B |
| Series F | Early 2025 | $13B | $183B |
| Series G | Feb 2026 | $30B | $380B |

Series G lead investors: GIC, Coatue. Major backers include Google, Amazon, Sequoia, Spark, Founders Fund, QIA.

## Why It Matters

Anthropic's structure — PBC + safety mandate + proportional scaling policy — makes it distinctly different from other frontier AI labs. Understanding it explains why Claude behaves the way it does and why the RSP and Constitutional AI exist.
